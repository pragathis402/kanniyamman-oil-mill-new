Our Story

Rooted in Tradition

Sri Kanniyamman Oil Mill produces high quality traditional wood-pressed oils. Our oils are pure, natural, and chemical-free. We are committed to delivering healthy and authentic cooking oils directly to customers.

Using ancient wooden churners passed down through generations, we cold-press each batch slowly — retaining natural vitamins, antioxidants, and the rich flavor that modern refining destroys.
